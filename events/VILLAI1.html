VILLAI-1

Photography(On spot)

DATE: MARCH 2

TIME: 9AM-12PM

 RULES AND REGULATIONS

* TOPIC WILL BE GIVEN ONSPOT.
* IT IS AN INDIVIDUAL EVENT.
* THE CONTEST CONSISTS OF 2 ROUNDS.
* PHOTOS SHOULD BE TAKEN WITH DSLR. USE OF MOBILE PHONESARE STRICTLY RESTRICTED.
* FOR PRELIMS: PARTICIPANT CAN SUBMIT A MAX. OF 3 PHOTOS.
* FOR FINALS: PARTICIPANT SHOULD SUBMIT ONLY ONE PHOTO.
* PHOTOS MUST BE SUBMITTED IN .JPEG OR .JPG FORMAT.
* EDITING,LOGO,WATERMARKS ARE NOT ALLOWED. EXCEPT CROPPING AND EXPOSURE CORRECTIONS [BRIGHTNESS AND CONTRAST]
* PHOTOS MUST BE RELEVANT TO THE THEME.
* PHOTOS MUST BE SUBMITTED WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME.
* PHOTOS MUST CONTAIN EXIF INFORMATION.
* DECISIONS TAKEN BY THE JUDGES ARE FINAL.

* PHOTOS WILL BE JUDGED ON THE BASIS OF:
-> THEME RELEVANCE.
-> PERSPECTIVE AND FRAMING.
-> CREATIVITY.
-> THE STORY THAT A PHOTO DEPICTS.
* PARTICIPANTS SHOULD NOT POST THE SUBMITTED PHOTO IN SOCIAL MEDIA UNTIL THE RESULTS ARE PUBLISHED.
* UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCE, ENTRIES ONCE SUBMITTED CANNOT BE TAKEN BACK FOR MODIFICATIONS.

"The art of photography is all about directing the attention of the viewer”. Here is the time for you budding photographers! Let people admire your photographs! Capture beauty around you but be sure not to get diverted from the theme. And, we are here for you guys, to appreciate the talent of photography in you. So, come disclose your talents to us!
